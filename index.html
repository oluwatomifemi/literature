Summary

Offred is a handmaid in the republic of Gilead, an autocratic state in what was once the United States of America. This nation was established by the Sons of Jacob, a group rooted in the Christian religion, following their attack on the politicians. The sons of Jacob shot the president and machine-gunned the congress. They blamed the Islamic fanatics for the assassination and declared a state of emergency. They put a hold to the constitution with the promise that it will be temporary. However, after they had consolidated more power, they abolished the old constitution and restructured the country to fit their ideals. In this new world, the rights of some groups were limited, especially those of the women.
